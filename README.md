# Web3 Security & Protocol Risk Resources

A curated collection of practical methodologies for navigating the intersection of institutional security and decentralized finance (DeFi).

## 🛡️ Focus Areas
- **Institutional Trust Frameworks:** Bridging enterprise GRC (NIST/FFIEC) with Web3-native risks.
- **Protocol Risk Assessments:** Checklists for evaluating DeFi protocol safety and smart contract interaction risk.
- **Incident Response Playbooks:** Workflows for on-chain incident command and post-incident stabilization.
- **DeFi Guardrails:** Practical security models for HNWIs and Family Offices.

## 📋 Ongoing Research & Mapping
- **Regulatory Mapping:** Aligning NYDFS Part 500 and MAS guidelines to on-chain controls.
- **Yield Risk Analysis:** Evaluating decomposition risks in liquid staking and lending protocols.
- **IAM for Web3:** Managing privileged access and multisig governance in institutional environments.
